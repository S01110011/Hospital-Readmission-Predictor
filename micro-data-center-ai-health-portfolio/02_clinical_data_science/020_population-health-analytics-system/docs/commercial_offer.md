# Commercial Offer

Project: Population Health Analytics System

## Offer

Population Health Analytics System can be sold as a paid discovery workshop, a fixed-scope MVP, a managed API, an executive dashboard add-on and a monthly compliance/monitoring retainer.

## Ideal Buyers

Hospital executives, clinical quality teams and operations leaders.

## Packaging

- Discovery workshop.
- Two-week proof of concept.
- Managed API deployment.
- Executive dashboard.
- Monitoring and compliance retainer.

## Success Metrics

- Reduced manual analysis time.
- Faster operational decision-making.
- Improved audit readiness.
- Clear SLA and support boundaries.
